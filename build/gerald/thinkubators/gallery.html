    We’ve got just the space for you.

    The Thinkubator is known for being a ‘chameleon’ space — we have a wide array of experience catering to different needs, arrangements, and groups. Whether you’re looking for a cozy meeting, group brainstorming session, or full-fledged event with 40+ people, we’ve got the space for you. More than just a stunning loft area, The Thinkubator contains a myriad of comfortable, stylish, and unique furniture to provide your attendees with the atmosphere most fitting for your event.

    Expand your mind with 360° views on our Rooftop Deck.

    What better way to break out of the box than to ascend to our 6th-floor rooftop deck with some of the best skyline views in the City of Chicago? With seating and tables for 40+ people, we’re primed for all sorts of warm weather entertainment.
